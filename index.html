About Us

Founded in 2021, AeroSim Solutions is an Indian aerospace technology startup dedicated to revolutionizing the field of aerodynamic simulations and precision instrumentation. We bring advanced solutions to the aerospace and defense sectors through expert knowledge in subsonic and supersonic flow regimes.

Our core capabilities include Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), wind tunnel data interpretation, and the custom manufacturing of pressure probes for experimental applications. We pride ourselves on bridging the gap between theory and application, delivering real-world value through engineering accuracy and scientific integrity.

Whether you're an academic institution conducting fundamental research or an aerospace company optimizing flight systems, AeroSim Solutions provides simulation-backed manufacturing and consulting services tailored to your goals. Our collaborative approach ensures that every project is supported with the highest quality analysis, rapid prototyping, and professional documentation.

Backed by a team of dedicated engineers and researchers, we continuously strive to stay at the forefront of aerospace innovation. From wind tunnel probe calibration to shockwave prediction in supersonic flows, our solutions have been trusted across numerous institutions and prototype programs.

Data Analysis & Reporting

Key Projects

Supersonic Intake Simulation

Developed a robust simulation model for analyzing shockwave patterns and pressure recovery in supersonic intake ducts for unmanned aerial systems.

Wind Tunnel Pressure Probe Calibration

Manufactured and calibrated multi-hole pressure probes for subsonic wind tunnel applications across several academic institutions in India.

Flight-Scale Instrumentation Design

Designed aerodynamic instrumentation for small UAVs to collect real-time pressure data during test flights in mixed flow regimes.
